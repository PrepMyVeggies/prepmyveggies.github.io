This base is designed to make everyday Indian cooking fast and flexible using PrepMyVeggies pre-cut packs.

## What’s in the pack
- Diced onion  
- Chopped tomato  
- Ginger  
- Garlic  

Optional add-ons (packed separately):
- Green chilli  
- Cilantro  

## How to use
Heat oil in a pan, add the masala base, and cook on medium heat for 5–7 minutes until fragrant and slightly reduced.

## Recipe ideas
**Paneer Masala**  
Add turmeric, chilli powder, coriander powder, paneer, and water. Simmer 5–7 minutes.

**Chana Masala**  
Add spices, cooked chickpeas, and water. Simmer until thick.

**Vegetable Curry**  
Add mixed vegetables and spices, cook until tender.

## Storage
Keep refrigerated. Best used within 3–4 days.
