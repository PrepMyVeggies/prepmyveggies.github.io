A classic French vegetable base used in soups, stews, and sauces.

## What’s in the pack
- Finely chopped onion  
- Finely chopped carrot  
- Finely chopped celery  

## How to use
Cook slowly in oil or butter on low heat until soft and aromatic.

## Recipe ideas
**Soup Base**  
Start any vegetable or lentil soup with this mix.

**Sauce Foundation**  
Use for pasta sauces, stews, or risotto.

## Storage
Keep refrigerated. Best used within 3–4 days.
