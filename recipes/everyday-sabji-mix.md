A flexible vegetable mix for everyday Indian dry or semi-dry dishes.

## What’s in the pack
- Potato  
- Carrot  
- Cauliflower florets  
- Cut green beans  

## How to use
Heat oil, add cumin or mustard seeds, then vegetables and spices. Cook covered until tender.

## Recipe ideas
**Aloo Gobi Style Sabji**  
Add turmeric, chilli powder, and coriander powder. Finish with garam masala.

**Simple Stir Sabji**  
Cook with minimal spices, salt, and oil for a light meal.

## Storage
Keep refrigerated. Best used within 3–4 days.
