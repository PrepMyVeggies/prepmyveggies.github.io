A classic mix for quick Mexican-style meals.

## What’s in the pack
- Sliced bell peppers  
- Sliced onions  

## How to use
Cook on high heat with oil, salt, and fajita seasoning until slightly charred.

## Recipe ideas
**Vegetable Fajitas**  
Serve with tortillas, beans, and cheese.

**Rice or Bowl Base**  
Add to rice bowls with salsa and protein of choice.

## Storage
Keep refrigerated. Best used within 3–4 days.
